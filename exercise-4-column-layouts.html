Exercise 4 – Column Layouts & Grid Classes

Exercise 4.1 – Sidebar (col-md-3) + Content Area (col-md-9)

A classic 3/9 split. Stacks vertically on small screens.

Sidebar
  • 🏠 Home
  • 📄 About
  • 📦 Products
  • 📞 Contact

Main Content Area

This section uses col-md-9 — 9 out of 12 columns wide on medium+ screens.

The sidebar takes col-md-3 — 3 out of 12 columns. Together they sum to 12.

3 + 9 = 12 ✅ — always make sure columns add up!
