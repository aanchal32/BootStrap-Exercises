Exercise 16 – Positioning Utilities

Exercise 16.1 – Fixed Footer

👇 See the dark bar at the bottom of the viewport — that's the fixed footer. It stays in place even when you scroll. 
<footer class="position-fixed bottom-0 start-0 end-0 bg-dark text-white">
  Fixed Footer
</footer>
Other position-fixed examples:
ClassesEffect
position-fixed top-0 start-0 end-0Fixed navbar at top
position-fixed bottom-0 end-0Floating action button
position-sticky top-0Sticky header (scrolls with page, then sticks)

Exercise 16.2 – Badge Overlay Using position-relative & position-absolute

The badge is absolutely positioned inside a relative container.

Translate helpers (centre-align badges):
<div class="position-relative">
  <img src="..." class="rounded-circle"/>
  <span class="position-absolute top-0 start-100 translate-middle badge bg-danger">5</span>
</div>
💡 translate-middle shifts the badge by -50% X and -50% Y, perfectly centering it on the corner point. Use translate-middle-x or translate-middle-y for one axis only.
